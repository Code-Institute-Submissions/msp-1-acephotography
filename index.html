How true Passion became a Profession

Despite an innate talent in accounting, Ashwin’s true love never was numbers, it was pictures. It’s difficult to say which came first, the fascination for creepy-crawlies or the obsession with photographing them. After 6 years of wriggling through bushes, tracking some scary looking creepy-crawlies, Ashwin finally realised that insects are notoriously unreliable pay masters, and has over the last few years, begrudgingly at first, diversified into various other fields.

Ashwin, during this transition process, completed a higher national diploma in photography in order to get as good at taking pictures of other things as he is at taking pictures of insects (they're not bugs).

Now he takes pictures at events (where they feed him veg biriyani), pictures of cool things that people aim to sell, and pictures of people, small to large, who want to look nice in pictures. As it turns out, he has quite a flair for it.

You can contact Ashwin for commercial product photography, coverage at events and personalised portraits using the various social links, or send an enquiry his way using the form in the contact section.
